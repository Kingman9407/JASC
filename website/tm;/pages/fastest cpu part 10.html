LPDDR5/LPDDR5X IS BETTER THAN YOU THINK :
#6 13th October 2024

LPDDR5/LPDDR5X is Better than you think:

LPDDR5 /LPDDR5X replacing DDR5 in laptops is a rising trend driven by the need for better power efficiency and compact designs. LPDDR5 is becoming the preferred choice for premium Slim laptops.

    Things to Know Before You Buy a Laptop with LPDDR5

  • - DDR5 is faster than LPDDR5/LPDDR5X, but the difference is unnoticeable in low to mid-range laptops.
  • - LPDDR5/LPDDR5X is soldered to the laptop motherboard and cannot be upgraded.
  • - LPDDR5/LPDDR5X is more expensive than DDR5.
  • - LPDDR5X consumes less power and very low power at idle.
  • - It is worth noting that high-performance laptops like Asus A14 (Ryzen 7 8845HS, RTX 4050) have 16GB LPDDR5X in dual channel.
  • - DDR5 is easier to repair and replace.

LPDDR5 VS DDR5 :

Feature DDR5 LPDDR5
Power Consumption Higher (1.1V–1.2V) Lower (1.05V or less)
Speed Up to 8400 MT/s Up to 6400 MT/s (8533 MT/s for LPDDR5X)
Form Factor Larger DIMMs (user-replaceable) Soldered to the motherboard (non-upgradable)
Battery Life Impact Shorter battery life Longer battery life due to power efficiency
Thermal Efficiency Generates more heat Lower heat generation, better thermal management
Target Use Case Desktops, gaming laptops, workstations Ultraportables, premium laptops, high-performance laptops
Upgradability User-upgradable Non-upgradable
Cost Typically more expensive for higher-end specs Typically more cost-effective for premium ultrabooks
Performance Best suited for heavy task Adequate for performance, balanced with battery life

Slim Laptops:

Most of slim laptops like like Asus Zenbook S 14( Intel Core Ultra 7 258V),HP ENVY (Intel Core Ultra 5 ) uses LPPDR ram to reduce space and reduce the ideal battery consumption

LPDDR5 iGPU Perormance:

the integrated GPU tend to perform bit faster in LPDDR5 but performance is not directly from the LPDDR-ram but from but the way it implemented in motherboard by the manufacturer(can differ from manufacturer to manufacturer)

Conclusion:

LPDDR5 performs similarly to DDR5 in day-to-day tasks while being more energy-efficient. However, remember that LPDDR cannot be upgraded, so it’s recommended to purchase at least 16GB.
