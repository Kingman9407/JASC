MOTOROLA EDGE 50 FUSION PHONE REVIEW:
#3 7th October 2024
The Motorola Edge 50 Fusion stands out as a mid-range smartphone offering a good balance of performance, display quality, and camera features, with a few trade-offs.

Specifications

Performance

Powered by Snapdragon 7s Gen 2 and 12GB RAM, it handles multitasking and gaming well it comes in Two memory configuration 8GB/128GB and 12GB/256GB

Battery

With a 4500mAh battery and 125W fast charging, it powers up quickly. Wireless charging adds to the convenience​

Software

Running Android 14 with a clean interface, it includes eSIM support, and have 3 major Android Updates
Motorola Edge 50 Fusion Colours

Display

The 6.7-inch AMOLED screen delivers smooth visuals with its 144Hz refresh rate, though it lacks HDR10+ support. The curved edges add a premium feel but may lead to accidental touches​

Camera

The 50MP main camera excels in bright light, producing vibrant images, but the colors can appear overly saturated. Night mode performs well in low light, while the 32MP selfie camera is decent​

Body

the phone is slim as 8mm a aluminim back and frame while its slim build weighs 199.5g, offering a comfortable user experience.
