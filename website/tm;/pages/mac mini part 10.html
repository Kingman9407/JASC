MAC mini M4

Apple launched the Mac Mini on November 1, 2024, featuring the M4 chip, developed by Apple Silicon. Starting at ₹59,900, it is one of the best productivity computers available.

M4 performance

The M4 Mac Mini consists of a 10-core CPU, a 10-core GPU, and 16GB of unified RAM, shared between the CPU and GPU. It offers a significant performance boost over Apple’s M1 chip. In Geekbench, the M4 scored 3826 in single-core and 14,990 in multi-core, making its single-core performance faster than any Intel or AMD CPU currently on the market.

Size and design

pic of M4 mac mini
The Mac Mini is a compact, fully functional computer with dimensions of just 5 inches in width and breadth, and 2 inches in height (5X5X2 inches). , providing powerful computing capabilities without taking up much space.

The Catch of M4 MAC mini

The base model of the Mac Mini offers better value compared to many Windows PCs, but it comes with a storage limitation of 256GB. While this may not be an issue for everyone, it's worth noting that many modern applications require more storage for installation and optimal performance. Users with higher storage needs might need to consider external storage or upgrading to a higher-capacity model.
